About Me

My name is Katarzyna Mikołajewska and I have been working with language, communication, and people for years.

I am a Norwegian language translator (I also work in Polish–English language pairs), a language instructor, and a mediator. I completed a degree in Scandinavian Studies at the University of Gdańsk, followed by Applied Sociology and Cultural Anthropology at the University of Warsaw, specialising in mediation and negotiation. I am currently developing my expertise in the area of mental health and crisis intervention.

As part of my personal development, I completed a year-long Mediation Studies programme based on Nonviolent Communication (NVC), which significantly deepened my understanding of conflict — in particular its sources, dynamics, and the role of unmet needs. My approach is rooted in the conviction that effective communication is not just about knowing the language, but above all about understanding the context, emotions, and relationships between people.

For over 10 years I have been working in an international environment — in Poland and abroad, including Norway — using English and Norwegian in my professional work. I carry out written and spoken translations, conduct language classes, and support clients in situations requiring precise and empathetic understanding.

As a language instructor, I have been teaching Norwegian for many years at various proficiency levels (A1–C1). I believe in an individualised approach and tailor the learning process to the needs, goals, and working style of each person, combining language development with building confidence in communication.

I also work in a non-governmental organisation, where I manage projects related to mental health, anti-discrimination, and social education. In my work I also draw on knowledge and experience gained through training in countering disinformation, public communication, and preventing violence and workplace bullying.

In my work I bring together:

  • language translations and international experience,
  • knowledge of communication and mediation,
  • experience of working with people across different cultural contexts.

I believe that good communication can genuinely change reality — it helps build understanding, reduce tensions, and open new possibilities.

If you are looking for a Norwegian language translator, want to learn a language, or need support in communication — feel free to get in touch.
