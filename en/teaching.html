Who are my classes for?

For me, teaching a language is not just about passing on knowledge - it's about building confidence and ease in communication in real-life and professional situations.

My classes are designed for learners at all levels, from complete beginners to advanced.

I work with both children and adults, especially those who:

  • want to start learning Norwegian from scratch
  • are preparing to move to Norway (for work or everyday life)
  • need Norwegian for professional purposes
  • want to overcome the speaking barrier and gain confidence in communication
  • are preparing for job interviews in Norwegian

I have experience in international recruitment, including sectors such as offshore, onshore, engineering, and oil & gas. This allows me to support students not only in learning the language, but also in preparing for job interviews and real professional situations.

Each programme is tailored to your individual goals and learning pace.

My teaching approach

Classes are interactive and conducted in a calm, supportive atmosphere.

I combine:

  • grammar learning
  • vocabulary development
  • practical communication (speaking and listening)
  • elements of Norwegian culture and everyday life

I place strong emphasis on speaking and comprehension in real-life situations, including professional contexts such as job interviews, workplace communication, and everyday interactions.

I use modern learning materials as well as my own exercises, always tailored to the student's needs.

I offer both online classes and in-person lessons in the Tricity (Gdańsk) area.

Each lesson is individually prepared based on your goals, level, and learning style.

I offer individual lessons (1:1), as well as lessons in pairs or small groups.

Get in touch and we'll create a learning plan tailored to your goals.
