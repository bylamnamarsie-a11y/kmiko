For Whom

For me, teaching a language is not just about conveying knowledge, but above all about building confidence in communication in specific life and professional situations. My classes are designed for people at all levels, from beginners to advanced learners.

I work with both children and adults, in particular those who:

  • want to start learning Norwegian from scratch
  • are preparing for a move to Norway (work, everyday life)
  • need the language in their professional work
  • want to overcome the speaking barrier and gain confidence in communication
  • are preparing for job interviews in Norwegian

I have experience in recruitment work in an international environment, including sectors such as offshore, onshore, engineering, and the oil & gas industry. Thanks to this, I also support people applying for jobs by helping them prepare linguistically for interviews and real professional situations.

Each programme is tailored to individual goals and learning pace.

How Classes Work

Classes are interactive and conducted in a calm, supportive atmosphere.

I combine:

  • grammar teaching
  • vocabulary development
  • practical communication (speaking and comprehension)
  • elements of culture and everyday life in Norway

I place great emphasis on speaking and comprehension in real situations, including a professional context — such as job interviews, communication at work, and everyday interactions.

Materials and Format

I use modern educational materials and my own exercises tailored to the student's needs.

I offer classes:

  • online
  • in-person (Tricity / Trójmiasto area)

Each lesson is prepared individually based on goals, level, and learning style.

I offer:

  • individual lessons (1:1)
  • possibility of lessons in pairs or small groups (to be agreed)
  • frequency and schedule tailored to the student
