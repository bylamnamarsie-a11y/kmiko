Interpreting Services

After studying in Norway, I gained experience in medical interpreting, and later in other fields as well. Thanks to many years of work in recruitment — including project management and conducting job interviews primarily in Norwegian and English — I am well acquainted with the business environment and processes, and feel comfortable communicating in both languages.

I provide:

  • consecutive interpreting (meetings, conversations, consultations)
  • simultaneous interpreting (events, training sessions)

I work both online and on-site.

I support communication in situations requiring precision and attentiveness, ensuring faithful rendering of content, intent, and context.

Written Translations

I provide written translations from Norwegian to Polish and from Polish to Norwegian.

I translate, among others:

  • official and private documents
  • informational and practical texts
  • correspondence
  • website content

Each text is prepared with attention to its purpose, style, and cultural context.

I do not provide certified (sworn) translations.

How I Work

My work is based on careful understanding of context, culture, and specialised terminology.

A translator's task is to faithfully convey what has been said or written — preserving meaning, tone, and intent.

I guarantee reliability, timely delivery, and full confidentiality of all entrusted materials and conversations. Each project is treated individually.

Terms of Cooperation and Pricing

The billing page for a translation is 1,800 characters with spaces in the source text.

Texts shorter than 1,800 characters are counted as a full page.

For specialised texts, the rate is agreed individually.

Standard rates:

  • from Norwegian to Polish — 80 PLN / page

The prices above apply to the standard service (up to 3 business days).
