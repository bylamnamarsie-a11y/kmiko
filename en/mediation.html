How I can help

I offer mediation support in family, workplace, private, and civil conflicts.

I help parties pause and truly hear one another, creating the conditions for solutions based on understanding rather than escalation.

I work using empathic communication, supporting:

  • identifying needs and emotions
  • bringing clarity to complex situations
  • rebuilding dialogue between parties

My role is not to impose solutions, but to create a space in which mutual understanding can emerge.

Who it is for

Mediation is available for:

  • couples and families
  • workplace teams
  • individuals involved in private or professional disputes

I particularly support situations where:

  • communication has become difficult
  • parties hold different perspectives or values
  • there is an intercultural context
  • conversations are affected by strong emotions or past experiences

How I work

I create a safe and structured space for dialogue, where each person has the opportunity to speak and be heard.

I draw on empathic communication as well as knowledge of conflict processes and social dynamics.

I ensure neutrality, confidentiality, and balance between all parties involved.

Training and workshops

I also design and deliver training sessions and workshops for:

  • schools and young people
  • teachers and educators
  • organisations and teams

Topics include:

  • communication and empathic listening
  • building understanding in conflict situations
  • preventing peer violence
  • developing dialogue and cooperation skills

All sessions are practical and tailored to the specific needs of the group.

If you are facing a conflict or would like to strengthen communication within your team or community, feel free to reach out.
