How I Can Help

I offer mediation support in family, workplace, private, and civil conflicts.

I help the parties pause and truly hear each other, so that a solution based on understanding — rather than escalation — becomes possible.

I work using empathic communication, supporting:

  • naming needs and emotions
  • bringing clarity to difficult situations
  • rebuilding dialogue between parties

My goal is not to impose solutions, but to create a space in which understanding can emerge.

For Whom

Mediation is available for:

  • couples and families
  • work teams
  • individuals involved in private and professional disputes

I particularly support situations in which:

  • communication difficulties arise
  • parties have different perspectives or values
  • there is an intercultural context
  • conversation is hindered by emotions or past experiences

How I Work

I create a safe and structured space for conversation, in which each party has the opportunity to speak and be heard.

I use tools of empathic communication and knowledge of conflict work and social processes.

I ensure neutrality, confidentiality, and balance between the parties.

Training and Workshops

I also conduct training and workshops for:

  • schools and young people
  • teachers and educators
  • organisations and teams

Topics covered include, among others:

  • communication and empathic listening
  • building understanding in conflict situations
  • preventing peer violence
  • developing dialogue and cooperation skills

Workshops are practical in nature and tailored to the group's needs.

If you need support in a conflict or want to strengthen communication skills in your team or environment, write to me.
